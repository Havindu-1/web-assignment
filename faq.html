FREQUENT QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other online payment methods.
How can I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support via email at support@gamestore.com or through our contact form on the website.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer refunds within 30 days of purchase, provided the game has not been downloaded or activated.
Can I play games on multiple devices?
Yes, most of our games can be played on multiple devices, but please check the specific game’s policy for details.
Are there any discounts for students?
Yes, we offer a 10% discount for students. Please verify your student status at checkout.
